About CoBo

My name is Stuart, and I’m currently studying to become a counsellor. Before this it was personal, shaped by lived experience with addiction, suicide, and dealing with the complexity of life.

CoBo began as a way to put into words some of what I’ve learned along the way from studies, conversations, grief, hope, and learning how to truly listen.

It is a space built on the combination of the concepts of Co: Contentment, Commitment, and Coping alongside Bo: Body, Boldness, and Boundaries. These ideas shape how we move, heal, and connect with both ourselves and others.
