Why I started Co&Bo

Co&Bo is a personal project born from my journey as a counselling student and my experiences with addiction and suicide. This platform serves as a space to share the lessons I've learned, the challenges I've faced, and the insights I've gained along the way.

The name "Co&Bo" reflects the essence of this journey, coaching and bonding, two fundamental aspects of the therapeutic process. It's about collaboration, connection, and the shared human experience.

Through this site, I hope to provide a gentle, supportive environment where individuals can find solace, understanding, and encouragement. Whether you're seeking information, inspiration, or a sense of community, Co&Bo is here for you.
