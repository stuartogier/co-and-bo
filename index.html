Welcome to CoBo

CoBo is a quiet corner of the internet, created to share what I’ve learned, and am still learning, as a counselling student. This space reflects my journey through the world of addiction and suicide with the moments of growth in between.

Here, you’ll find honest reflections, small insights, and thoughtful resources. Whether you’re navigating your own healing, supporting someone else, or simply curious.

I hope something here resonates with you.
